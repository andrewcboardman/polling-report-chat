Poll Name: Homeless and Police PR - W184
Table Number: 14
Question: Q3_4. Policing major events i.e. football matches: To what extent do you think that, generally, the police in the UK do a good or bad job at each of the following?
Summary: The data shows the responses from 2,235 respondents in the UK on their perception of how well the police handle major events like football matches. The majority (70%) believe the police do a good job, with 16% saying "very good job" and 54% saying "fairly good job". However, 21% think the police do a bad job, with 14% saying "fairly bad job" and 7% saying "very bad job". The remaining 9% responded "Don't know".
