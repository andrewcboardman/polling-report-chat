Poll Name: Ongoing strike action by rail staff

Table Number: Table 19

Question: QTPW177Q5_4. Ongoing strike action by rail staff: Thinking about each of the following, how would it impact your attitude towards the party that you felt was responsible?

Data Summary: The data presents the responses from 2,100 respondents to a question about how ongoing rail staff strike action would impact their attitude towards the party they felt was responsible. The responses are broken down by gender, age, region, social grade, 2019 past vote, and 2016 EU referendum vote. The data shows that 21% would stop voting for the responsible party, 18% would still vote but feel more negatively, 24% would still vote with no change in view, and 9% would still vote and feel more positively. 28% responded "Don't know".
