Poll Name: Transport Podcast
Table Number: Table 15

Question: QTPW177Q5. SUMMARY: Thinking about each of the following, how would it impact your attitude towards the party that you felt was responsible?

Summary:
The data presents the responses of 2,100 respondents to various transport-related policies and their potential impact on voting attitudes. It covers topics such as the expansion of the Ultra-Low Emission Zone in London, speed limits, rail infrastructure projects, strikes, ticket office closures, air traffic disruptions, bans on fossil fuel vehicles and boilers, and energy efficiency requirements for rental properties. The responses range from stopping voting for the responsible party to feeling more positive towards them, providing insights into public opinion on these transportation and environmental issues.
