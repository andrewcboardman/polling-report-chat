Question 2: Which of the following is closest to your view?

The data comes from a poll on homelessness and views on whether it is a lifestyle choice or not. The CSV file contains responses from 2,235 respondents across various demographic groups such as gender, age, region, and voting preference.

The poll results show that the majority (75%) of respondents believe that homelessness is not a lifestyle choice, while 16% consider it a lifestyle choice, and 9% responded with "Don't know." The responses varied across different demographic groups, with certain groups being more likely to view homelessness as a lifestyle choice compared to others.
