Purpose: This question aims to understand if low-income households have taken on new personal loans from banks or building societies in the last six months, or if they had existing loans that have increased or stayed the same in size.

Key findings:
- 5% of respondents reported this as a new debt in the last six months.
- 7% said they had this debt before November 2022, and the size has increased. 
- 9% had this debt before November 2022, but the size has not increased.
- 77% do not hold this type of debt.
- 2% responded "Don't know".
