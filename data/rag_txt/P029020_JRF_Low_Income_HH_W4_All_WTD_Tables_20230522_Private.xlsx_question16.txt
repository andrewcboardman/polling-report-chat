The question is from the file "P029020_JRF_Low_Income_HH_W4_All_WTD_Tables_20230522_Private.xlsx" and it is Question 24b.6.

The purpose of this question is to understand the working status of the sixth other adult in the household, if there are more than one adult in the household.

Key findings:
- Among the low-income households surveyed, only a small proportion (0.24%) reported having a sixth adult in the household.
- The majority of these sixth adults were either students (0.52%) or working full-time for an employer (0.24%).
- A small percentage were self-employed full-time (0.24%).
- None of the sixth adults were reported to be working part-time, seeking work, retired, or in other employment categories like zero-hour contracts or agency work.
