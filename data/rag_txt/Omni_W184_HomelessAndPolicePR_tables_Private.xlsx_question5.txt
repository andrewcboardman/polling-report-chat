Question text: Q1_3. In the UK generally: To what extent would you say that homelessness is, or is not, a problem in each of the following areas?

The purpose of this question is to gauge the public's perception of the extent of homelessness as a problem in the UK generally. The key findings from the data show that a significant majority (84%) of respondents consider homelessness to be a problem in the UK, with 44% viewing it as a "big problem" and 40% considering it "somewhat a problem." Only 12% believe that homelessness is not much of a problem or not a problem at all in the UK. The data highlights a widespread recognition of homelessness as a significant issue across the country.
