Question Number: 44
Question Text: Q44. Which of the following best describes where you live?

Purpose of the Question: This question aims to gather information about the type of residential area the respondents live in, such as a town, city, suburb, or rural area.

Key Findings:
- Nearly a third (31%) of respondents live in an urban area, with 14% residing in a city with a population of 1 million or more, and another 14% in a city with a population between 300,000 and 1 million.
- Over half (57%) of respondents live in intermediate areas, with 15% in the suburbs or outskirts of a big city, 13% in the suburbs or outside a town, and 12% in a town.
- The remaining 13% of respondents live in rural areas, with 12% residing in a country village and only 1% living on a farm or in the countryside.
- The data highlights the diversity of residential areas represented in the sample, providing insights into the living conditions and experiences of low-income households across different types of communities.
