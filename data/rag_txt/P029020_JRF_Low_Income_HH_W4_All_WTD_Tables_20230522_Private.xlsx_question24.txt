Purpose: This question asks respondents to provide their best estimate of their total monthly housing costs, including rental costs and/or mortgage payments (principal and interest), but excluding utility bills.

Key Findings:
- The mean monthly housing cost reported across all respondents was £601.63.
- Around 18% of respondents reported housing costs between £301-£500 per month.
- Around 13% reported costs between £501-£700 per month.
- Approximately 12% reported costs above £1,000 per month.
- There were some variations in reported housing costs based on factors like income level, tenure type, and region.
