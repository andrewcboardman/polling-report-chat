Question number: 7
Question text: Q7. Do you or any other member in your household receive any of the following benefits?

Purpose of the question: This question aims to gather information about the types of benefits received by the respondents or members of their households.

Key findings:
- Universal Credit (19%) and Child Benefit (18%) were the most commonly received benefits among the respondents.
- Other frequently received benefits included Council Tax Reduction/Rates Rebate (17%), Disability benefits (16%), Housing Benefit (14%), and Child Tax Credit (8%).
- A significant portion (44%) did not receive any of the listed benefits.
