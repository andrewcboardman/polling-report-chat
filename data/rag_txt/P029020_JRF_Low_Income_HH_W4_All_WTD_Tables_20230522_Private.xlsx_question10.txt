Question number: 24
Question text: Q24. Which of the following best describes your current working status?

Purpose of the question: This question aims to understand the employment status of the respondents, categorizing them into various working situations such as full-time employment, self-employment, part-time work, unemployment, retirement, or other non-working conditions.

Key findings:
- 31% of respondents were working for an employer full-time (30 hours or more per week).
- 4% were self-employed and working full-time (30 hours or more per week).
- 13% were working for an employer part-time (up to 29 hours per week).
- 3% were self-employed and working part-time (up to 29 hours per week).
- 4% were not working but seeking work or temporarily unemployed/sick.
- 3% were students.
- 28% were retired.
- 5% identified as house-wives/house-husbands (looking after the home).
- 5% were permanently sick or disabled.
- Less than 1% were temporarily sick with no job to go to, engaged in agency work or temporary contracts, zero-hours contracts, or other activities like voluntary work.
