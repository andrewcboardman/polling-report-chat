Question text: Q5_2. Environmental protests e.g. Just Stop Oil, Extinction Rebellion : To what extent do you think that the police handle each of the following types protests / demonstrations well or badly?

The purpose of this question is to gauge public perception of how well the police handle environmental protests and demonstrations, such as those organized by groups like Just Stop Oil and Extinction Rebellion. The key findings show a divided opinion, with 39% believing the police handle these protests well (9% very well, 30% fairly well), while 49% think they handle them badly (27% fairly badly, 22% very badly). There is also a notable proportion (12%) who responded "Don't know." The data provides insights into public attitudes towards policing of environmental activism in the UK.
