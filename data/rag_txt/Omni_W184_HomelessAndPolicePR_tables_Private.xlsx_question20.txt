Question text: Q5_1. Pro-Palestine protests : To what extent do you think that the police handle each of the following types protests / demonstrations well or badly?

The purpose of this question is to gauge public perception of how well or poorly the police handle pro-Palestine protests and demonstrations in the UK. The key findings show that 41% of respondents think the police handle these protests well (11% very well, 31% fairly well), while 40% think they handle them badly (22% fairly badly, 19% very badly). There is a notable divide in opinions, with similar proportions viewing the police's handling positively and negatively. Around 18% of respondents selected "Don't know" for this question.
