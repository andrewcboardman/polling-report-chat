Question number: 31
Question text: Q9a_5. Credit card (amount owed that you can't repay at the end of the month - not the maximum limit): For each of the following types of debt, please tell us whether it was a new debt for your household in the last six months (since November 2022) or if you already had it before November 2022, and if it has changed in size?

Purpose: This question aims to understand the prevalence and changes in credit card debt among low-income households in the last six months.

Key findings:
- 9% of respondents reported having a new credit card debt since November 2022.
- 12% of respondents had existing credit card debt before November 2022, and the size of the debt increased since then.
- 14% of respondents had existing credit card debt before November 2022, but the size of the debt remained the same or decreased.
- 62% of respondents did not have any credit card debt.
