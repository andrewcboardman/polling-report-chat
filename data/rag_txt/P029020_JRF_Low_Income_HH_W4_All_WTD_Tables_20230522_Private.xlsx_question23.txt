Question 41

Purpose: This question aims to understand the reasons why landlords or letting agents have contacted private and social renters in the last six months (since November 2022).

Key findings:
- The most common reason cited by 26% of renters was for their landlord/agent to discuss a rent increase and new tenancy agreement that the renter could afford.
- 9% of renters mentioned being contacted to provide assurance that they could stay in their home.
- 7% reported being contacted about a rent increase and new tenancy agreement that they could not afford.
- 5% indicated being threatened with eviction or receiving an eviction notice.
- 9% selected "Other" reasons for being contacted by their landlord/agent.
- 42% stated that their landlord/agent had not been in touch with them since November 2022.
