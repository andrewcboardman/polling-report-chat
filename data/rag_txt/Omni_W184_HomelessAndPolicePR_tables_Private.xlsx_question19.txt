Q5. Summary Badly: To what extent do you think that the police handle each of the following types protests / demonstrations well or badly?

The purpose of this question is to gauge public perception of how well or poorly the police handle various types of protests and demonstrations in the UK. The key findings show that a significant proportion of respondents believe the police handle certain protests badly, particularly environmental protests like Just Stop Oil and Extinction Rebellion (50%), pro-Palestine protests (40%), and Black Lives Matter protests (41%). The data also reveals differences in perceptions across demographic groups and political affiliations.
