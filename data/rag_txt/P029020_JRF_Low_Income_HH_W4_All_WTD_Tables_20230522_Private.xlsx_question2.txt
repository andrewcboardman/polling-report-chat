Question text: Q3. Where do you live?

Purpose: This question aims to gather information about the geographical location of the respondents, specifically which region or country within the UK they reside in.

Key findings:
- The respondents are spread across different regions of the UK, with the highest representation from the South East (13%), North West (12%), and London (10%).
- Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland account for 8%, 5%, and 2% of the respondents, respectively.
- Other regions like the North East, Yorkshire and the Humber, East Midlands, West Midlands, and Eastern England each have around 9% representation.
- The South West accounts for 9% of the respondents.
- A small percentage (less than 1%) of respondents reside outside the UK or in the Channel Islands.
