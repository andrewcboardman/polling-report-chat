Question number: 28
Question text: "Q9a_2. Personal loan from a payday lender: For each of the following types of debt, please tell us whether it was a new debt for your household in the last six months (since November 2022) or if you already had it before November 2022, and if it has changed in size?"

Purpose: The question aims to understand the prevalence and changes in payday loan debt among low-income households over the past six months.

Key findings:
- 4.4% of respondents reported having a new payday loan debt in the last six months (since November 2022).
- 5.6% of respondents had an existing payday loan debt before November 2022, and the size of the debt increased since then.
- 4.6% of respondents had an existing payday loan debt before November 2022, but the size of the debt did not increase (stayed the same or decreased) since then.
- The majority (83.3%) of respondents did not have any payday loan debt.
