Question 31: Age. How old are you?

Purpose: This question aims to gather information about the age distribution of the survey respondents.

Key Findings:
- The survey included respondents from various age groups, ranging from 18 to 65 and above.
- The largest age group represented was 35-54 years old, with 35-44 years (19%) and 45-54 years (17%) being the most prominent.
- The age group with the smallest representation was 65 and above (12%).
- The mean age of the respondents was 42.01 years.
