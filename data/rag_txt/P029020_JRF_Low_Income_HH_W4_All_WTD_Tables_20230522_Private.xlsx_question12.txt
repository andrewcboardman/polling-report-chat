Purpose: The question Q24b.2 asks about the working status of the second other adult in households with more than one adult.

Key Findings:
- The most common working status for the second adult is working for an employer full-time (30+ hours per week), at 35% of households.
- 13% of second adults work part-time for an employer (up to 29 hours per week).
- 6% of second adults are self-employed full-time, and 3% are self-employed part-time.
- 6% of second adults are not working but seeking work or temporarily unemployed/sick.
- 6% of second adults are retired.
