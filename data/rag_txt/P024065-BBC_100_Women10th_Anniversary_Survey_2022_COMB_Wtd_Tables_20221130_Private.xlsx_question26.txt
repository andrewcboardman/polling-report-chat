Question 26

The purpose of this question is to understand if women feel more or less comfortable discussing gender-based crimes (e.g., domestic abuse, forced marriage, female genital mutilation) with various individuals or platforms compared to 10 years ago.

Key findings:
- 68% of respondents feel more comfortable discussing gender-based crimes with a friend, while 9% feel less comfortable.
- 65% feel more comfortable discussing with a partner, while 10% feel less comfortable.
- 64% feel more comfortable discussing anonymously on social media, while 14% feel less comfortable.
- 63% feel more comfortable discussing with a family member, while 12% feel less comfortable.
- 51% feel more comfortable discussing with the police, while 20% feel less comfortable.
