Question number: 10_5
Question text: "With the police: Compared with 10 years ago (i.e. 2012), do you think women in your country feel more or less comfortable discussing a gender-based crime (e.g. domestic abuse, forced marriage, female genital mutilation) with each of the following?"

The purpose of this question is to gauge whether women feel more or less comfortable discussing gender-based crimes with the police compared to 10 years ago. The key findings show that overall, 51% of respondents feel more comfortable discussing such crimes with the police, while 20% feel less comfortable. There are variations across countries, with higher proportions feeling more comfortable in countries like Brazil (60%), China (70%), and Kenya (64%), while lower proportions feel more comfortable in countries like Pakistan (28%), Russia (26%), and Saudi Arabia (62% feel about the same).
