Question Text: Q4. Which of the following categories would best describe your ethnicity?

Purpose: This question aims to gather information about the ethnic backgrounds of the survey respondents.

Key Findings: The data shows that the majority of respondents (84%) identified as White (British/English/Welsh/Scottish/Northern Irish/British, Irish, Gypsy or Irish Traveller, or Any other White background). The second largest ethnic group was Asian or Asian British (6%), followed by Black or Black British (5%), and Mixed/Multiple ethnic groups (4%). A small percentage (1%) identified as belonging to Other ethnic groups or preferred not to disclose their ethnicity.
