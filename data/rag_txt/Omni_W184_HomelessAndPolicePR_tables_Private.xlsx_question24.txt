Question text: Q5_5. Anti-Brexit protests : To what extent do you think that the police handle each of the following types protests / demonstrations well or badly?

The purpose of this question is to gauge public perception of how well the police handle anti-Brexit protests and demonstrations in the UK. The key findings are:

- 46% of respondents believe the police handle anti-Brexit protests well (11% very well, 35% fairly well).
- 35% believe the police handle anti-Brexit protests badly (23% fairly badly, 12% very badly).
- 20% responded that they don't know.
- There are notable differences in perception across demographics such as age, region, and political affiliation.
