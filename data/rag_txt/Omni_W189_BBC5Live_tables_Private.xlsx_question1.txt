Question text: Q2. You mentioned that you have experienced or witnessed anti-social behaviour in your local area. Has this behaviour ever made you feel any of the following?

Purpose: This question aims to understand the emotional impact of witnessing or experiencing anti-social behaviour on individuals in their local area.

Key findings: The data reveals that a significant proportion (45%) of respondents felt angry or annoyed due to anti-social behaviour. Additionally, 30% reported feeling unsafe walking alone in their area, 18% felt frightened, and 12% felt unsafe in their homes. Notably, 27% did not experience any of the listed emotional impacts. The responses varied across different demographic groups, such as gender, age, and region.
