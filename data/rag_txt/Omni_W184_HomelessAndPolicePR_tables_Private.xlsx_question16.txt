Question 17: Q4. Which of the following is closest to your view? 'The police in the UK...

Purpose: This question aims to gauge the public's perception of potential biases within the UK police force, specifically whether they are perceived as having a right-wing, left-wing, or no political bias.

Key Findings:
- The largest proportion (34%) of respondents felt that the police do not have a right or left-wing bias.
- 26% perceived the police as having a right-wing bias (17% slight, 9% strong).
- 12% perceived the police as having a left-wing bias (6% slight, 5% strong).
- A significant portion (28%) responded "Don't know," indicating uncertainty or lack of a strong opinion on this matter.
