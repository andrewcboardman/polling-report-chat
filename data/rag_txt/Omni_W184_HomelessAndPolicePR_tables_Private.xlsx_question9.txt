Question text: Q3. Summary Bad job: To what extent do you think that, generally, the police in the UK do a good or bad job at each of the following?

The purpose of this question is to gauge public perception of the areas in which the police in the UK are perceived to be doing a bad job. The key findings from the data indicate that the highest percentage of respondents (59%) believe the police are doing a bad job at investigating minor crimes and having a visible presence in their area. Additionally, around half (51%) think the police are doing a bad job at acting as a deterrent for criminal activity. The data suggests that the public perceives the police as underperforming in addressing minor crimes, maintaining visibility, and deterring criminal behavior.
