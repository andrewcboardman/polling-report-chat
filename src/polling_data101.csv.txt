The poll is the BBC 100 Women 10th Anniversary Survey 2022, Table 20, Question 9. The data is from a survey asking respondents "Compared with 10 years ago (i.e. 2012), do you feel more or less safe expressing your honest opinions online in your country?". The CSV file contains responses from 15,723 respondents across 18 countries, categorized by demographics such as age, working status, relationship status, etc. It provides the weighted percentages for each response option (Much safer, Slightly safer, About the same, Slightly less safe, Much less safe, Don't know) broken down by country and demographic groups.
